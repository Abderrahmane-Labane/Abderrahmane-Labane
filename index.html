Mathematical Physics · Algebraic Topology · Geometry

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at Montana State University, Bozeman. My background spans theoretical physics and pure mathematics. My research interest is to study the topology and geometry of manifolds through the lens of algebraic topology, differential topology, and mathematical quantum field theory.
